# Tutor

## Module Overview

The Tutor module enables tutors to efficiently manage their daily academic responsibilities within the coaching institute.

Tutors are responsible for delivering classes, monitoring student progress, conducting assessments, maintaining attendance, and supporting students throughout their learning journey.

The objective of this module is to provide tutors with a simple and focused workspace that helps them perform all academic activities efficiently.

---

# Objectives

- Deliver quality academic sessions.
- Manage assigned batches and students.
- Conduct classroom and live classes.
- Track student attendance.
- Upload Learning Resources
- Conduct and evaluate assessments.
- Monitor student performance.
- Support student learning.

---

# Responsibilities

A Tutor is responsible for:

- Teaching assigned subjects.
- Managing assigned batches.
- Taking attendance.
- Conducting live classes.
- Uploading study materials.
- Creating assignments.
- Evaluating answer papers.
- Reviewing student performance.
- Supporting students academically.
