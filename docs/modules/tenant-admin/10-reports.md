# Reports & Analytics

## Module Overview

The Reports & Analytics module provides the coaching institute with meaningful insights into academic operations, student performance, tutor activities, and overall institute performance.

This module consolidates data from all major business modules and transforms it into actionable reports that help the institute make informed academic and operational decisions.

The objective of this module is to support data-driven decision making, improve academic quality, and continuously monitor the overall performance of the institute.

---

# Objectives

- Monitor institute performance.
- Generate operational reports.
- Track student progress.
- Analyze tutor performance.
- Monitor academic activities.
- Measure assessment outcomes.
- Support management decision making.
- Identify opportunities for academic improvement.

---

# Business Rules

- Reports should always use the latest available data.
- Analytics should be generated from verified reports.
- Reports should be available based on user permissions.
- Historical reports should remain accessible.
- Analytics should support institute-level decision making.
- Sensitive student information should be visible only to authorized users.
