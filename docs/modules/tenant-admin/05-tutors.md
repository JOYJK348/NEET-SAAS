# Tutor Management

## Module Overview

The Tutor Management module is responsible for managing the complete tutor lifecycle within the coaching institute.

This module covers tutor onboarding, subject assignment, batch allocation, academic responsibilities, student mentoring, performance monitoring, and overall academic delivery.

The objective of this module is to ensure that every tutor is properly assigned, efficiently managed, and contributes to delivering high-quality education throughout the student's academic journey.

---

# Objectives

- Manage the complete tutor lifecycle.
- Simplify tutor onboarding.
- Assign tutors to appropriate subjects and batches.
- Manage academic responsibilities.
- Monitor teaching activities.
- Track tutor performance.
- Ensure consistent academic delivery.

---

# Tutor Journey

The complete tutor journey consists of the following stages.

## 1. Tutor Onboarding

A tutor officially joins the coaching institute.

Purpose

- Register tutor information.
- Verify qualifications.
- Prepare the tutor for academic responsibilities.

---

## 2. Subject Assignment

The tutor is assigned one or more academic subjects.

Purpose

- Define teaching responsibilities.
- Allocate subject ownership.

Examples

- Science
- Math
- History
- Geography

---

## 3. Batch Assignment

The tutor is assigned to one or more student batches.

Purpose

- Organize teaching schedules.
- Allocate tutors based on institute requirements.

Examples

- Regular Course Morning Batch
- Regular Course Evening Batch
- Crash Course Batch

---

## 4. Academic Delivery

The tutor delivers academic sessions for assigned batches.

Activities

- Conduct Offline Classes
- Conduct Live Classes
- Explain Concepts
- Clear Student Doubts

---

## 5. Study Material Contribution

The tutor contributes learning resources for students.

Activities

- Upload Notes
- Upload PDFs
- Upload Assignments
- Share Practice Questions

---

## 6. Assessments

The tutor participates in student assessments.

Activities

- Conduct Mock Tests
- Review Student Responses
- Evaluate Performance

---

## 7. Evaluation

The tutor evaluates student performance.

Purpose

- Manual Paper Evaluation
- OMR Verification
- Marks Entry
- Provide Feedback

---

## 8. Student Mentoring

The tutor continuously guides students throughout the course.

Examples

- Identify weak students.
- Suggest improvement plans.
- Provide academic guidance.
- Encourage consistent learning.

---

## 9. Performance Review

The institute monitors tutor performance.

Examples

- Assigned Subjects
- Assigned Batches
- Classes Conducted
- Pending Evaluations
- Student Academic Progress

---

## 10. Exit / Deactivation

The tutor leaves the institute or becomes inactive.

Purpose

- Disable platform access.
- Preserve academic history.
- Maintain historical records.

---

# Tutor Responsibilities

Tutors are expected to:

- Teach assigned subjects.
- Conduct scheduled classes.
- Deliver live sessions.
- Upload study materials.
- Conduct mock tests.
- Evaluate student papers.
- Mentor assigned students.
- Support student academic growth.

---

# Business Rules

- Every tutor must be assigned at least one subject.
- A tutor may teach multiple batches.
- Multiple tutors may teach the same batch for different subjects.
- Tutors can access only their assigned students and batches.
- Tutors can evaluate only the assessments assigned to them.
- Tutor academic history should be preserved even after deactivation.
