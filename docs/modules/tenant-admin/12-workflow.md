# Workflow

## Module Overview

The Workflow defines the day-to-day operational flow followed by the Tenant Admin while managing the coaching institute.

It describes how academic, administrative, and operational activities are performed throughout the academic year to ensure smooth institute management.

The objective of this workflow is to standardize institute operations, improve efficiency, and maintain a consistent academic experience for students, tutors, and parents.

---

# Objectives

- Standardize daily institute operations.
- Ensure smooth academic management.
- Improve operational efficiency.
- Monitor institute performance.
- Support informed decision making.
- Deliver a consistent learning experience.

---

# Operational Principles

The Tenant Admin should

- Prioritize academic quality.
- Monitor institute activities daily.
- Resolve pending tasks promptly.
- Ensure timely communication.
- Continuously monitor student progress.
- Support tutors in academic delivery.
- Review institute performance regularly.
- Take corrective actions whenever required.

---

# Business Rules

- Academic activities should follow the approved timetable.
- Mock tests should follow the academic calendar.
- Results should be published only after evaluation.
- Students should receive appropriate study materials.
- Parent communication should be maintained regularly.
- Reports should be reviewed periodically.
- Institute settings should be updated only by the Tenant Admin.

---

# Success Indicators

A well-managed institute should demonstrate

- High Student Attendance
- Consistent Academic Progress
- Timely Syllabus Completion
- Regular Mock Tests
- Timely Result Publication
- Active Parent Engagement
- Efficient Tutor Management
- Continuous Academic Improvement
