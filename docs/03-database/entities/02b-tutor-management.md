# Tutor Management Domain

## Purpose

The Tutor Management domain is responsible for managing faculty members who deliver academic programs within the coaching institute.

This domain covers the complete tutor lifecycle, including onboarding, profile management, subject allocation, batch assignment, class delivery, attendance management, assessment evaluation, and academic performance monitoring.

The primary objective of this domain is to ensure that every tutor can efficiently manage teaching responsibilities while maintaining high academic standards across the institute.

---

# Objectives

- Manage tutor profiles.
- Organize tutor onboarding.
- Assign academic responsibilities.
- Manage subject and batch allocations.
- Support class delivery.
- Monitor teaching activities.
- Evaluate tutor performance.
- Maintain faculty records.

---

# Business Entities

The following business entities belong to the Tutor Management domain.

---

## Tutor

### Purpose

Represents a faculty member responsible for delivering academic instruction within the coaching institute.

### Business Responsibilities

- Conduct classes.
- Deliver academic content.
- Guide students.
- Evaluate assessments.
- Support academic excellence.

---

## Tutor Profile

### Purpose

Represents the complete professional profile of a tutor.

### Business Responsibilities

- Maintain personal information.
- Maintain professional qualifications.
- Maintain teaching experience.
- Maintain contact information.

---

## Tutor Assignment

### Purpose

Represents the academic responsibilities assigned to a tutor.

Assignments may include one or more courses, batches, subjects, and academic activities.

### Business Responsibilities

- Assign courses.
- Assign batches.
- Assign subjects.
- Manage teaching workload.

---

## Tutor Schedule

### Purpose

Represents the teaching schedule assigned to a tutor.

### Business Responsibilities

- Manage daily classes.
- Prevent schedule conflicts.
- Organize teaching activities.
- Support timetable planning.

---

## Tutor Attendance

### Purpose

Represents attendance records of tutors for scheduled academic sessions.

### Business Responsibilities

- Record attendance.
- Monitor punctuality.
- Support payroll and reporting.
- Maintain attendance history.

---

## Tutor Performance

### Purpose

Represents the overall academic contribution and effectiveness of a tutor.

### Business Responsibilities

- Monitor teaching performance.
- Review student outcomes.
- Evaluate class completion.
- Support academic quality improvement.

---

## Tutor Leave

### Purpose

Represents leave requests submitted by tutors.

### Business Responsibilities

- Apply for leave.
- Approve or reject requests.
- Manage substitute arrangements.
- Maintain leave history.

---

## Tutor Documents

### Purpose

Represents professional and administrative documents associated with tutors.

### Examples

- Educational Certificates
- Experience Certificates
- Identity Proof
- Employment Documents
- Contract Documents

### Business Responsibilities

- Store documents.
- Verify submitted documents.
- Maintain document history.

# Business Principles

- Every tutor should maintain a professional profile.
- Every tutor should have at least one academic assignment.
- Tutors should only access assigned academic resources.
- Teaching schedules should not conflict.
- Attendance should be maintained for every scheduled class.
- Tutor performance should be continuously monitored.
- Professional documents should remain securely maintained.
