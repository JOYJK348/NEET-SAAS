# Reporting & Analytics Domain

## Purpose

The Reporting & Analytics domain is responsible for collecting, organizing, analyzing, and presenting business and academic insights across the coaching institute.

This domain transforms operational data into meaningful reports and analytics that help institute administrators, tutors, and management make informed decisions.

The primary objective of this domain is to improve academic quality, operational efficiency, and business performance through data-driven decision making.

---

# Objectives

- Generate operational reports.
- Generate academic reports.
- Monitor institute performance.
- Analyze student performance.
- Track tutor performance.
- Measure academic effectiveness.
- Support management decision making.
- Maintain historical reporting.

---

# Business Entities

The following business entities belong to the Reporting & Analytics domain.

---

## Report

### Purpose

Represents a structured summary of business or academic information generated by the platform.

### Business Responsibilities

- Generate reports.
- Organize report data.
- Support decision making.
- Maintain reporting history.

---

## Dashboard

### Purpose

Represents a visual summary of key operational and academic metrics consumed by Users based on their role.

Dashboard visibility depends on User Role (Tenant Admin, Tutor, Student, Parent, etc.).

### Business Responsibilities

- Display institute KPIs.
- Highlight important activities.
- Monitor institute health.
- Support daily operations.
- Support role-based access (User role decides visibility).

---

## Analytics

### Purpose

Represents processed insights generated from operational and academic data.

### Business Responsibilities

- Identify trends.
- Measure performance.
- Support planning.
- Improve institute operations.

---

## Student Performance Report

### Purpose

Represents academic reports generated for individual students.

### Examples

- Subject Performance
- Attendance Summary
- Test Performance
- Progress Report
- Rank History

### Business Responsibilities

- Monitor student growth.
- Support parent communication.
- Identify improvement areas.

---

## Tutor Performance Report

### Purpose

Represents reports measuring teaching effectiveness and academic contribution.

### Examples

- Classes Conducted
- Attendance
- Subject Coverage
- Student Feedback
- Evaluation Completion

### Business Responsibilities

- Review faculty performance.
- Improve teaching quality.
- Support academic planning.

---

## Institute Performance Report

### Purpose

Represents overall operational and academic performance of the coaching institute.

### Examples

- Student Growth
- Batch Performance
- Course Performance
- Revenue Summary
- Admission Statistics

### Business Responsibilities

- Monitor institute growth.
- Evaluate operational efficiency.
- Support strategic planning.

---

## Attendance Report

### Purpose

Represents attendance statistics generated from Attendance data across students, tutors, batches, and courses.

### Business Responsibilities

- Monitor attendance trends.
- Identify irregular attendance.
- Support academic interventions.
- Generated FROM Attendance entity (source data).

---

## Assessment Report

### Purpose

Represents reports generated from Assessments (Mock Test, Weekly Test, Monthly Test, Grand Test, Unit Test).

### Examples

- Mock Test Analysis
- Weekly Test Analysis
- Monthly Test Analysis
- Grand Test Analysis
- Unit Test Analysis
- Rank Distribution
- Subject-wise Scores
- Accuracy Analysis

### Business Responsibilities

- Review assessment outcomes.
- Improve academic quality.
- Support revision planning.
- Generate reports per Assessment type.

---

## Audit Report

### Purpose

Represents historical records of important activities performed within the platform.

### Business Responsibilities

- Maintain operational transparency.
- Support compliance.
- Track administrative activities.

# Business Principles

- Reports should be generated from reliable business data.
- Dashboards should display real-time operational insights whenever possible.
- Historical reports should remain available for comparison.
- Reports should support business and academic decision making.
- Analytics should help identify trends and improvement opportunities.
- Reports should be accessible only to authorized users.
- The reporting system should support future business growth.
