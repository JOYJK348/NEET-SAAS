# Parent Management Domain

## Purpose

The Parent Management domain is responsible for managing parents and guardians associated with students enrolled in the coaching institute.

Parents play an important role in monitoring student attendance, academic performance, assessments, announcements, and overall progress. This domain ensures that parents remain connected with the institute throughout the student's academic journey.

The primary objective of this domain is to strengthen communication between the institute, parents, and students while improving transparency and student success.

---

# Objectives

- Manage parent information.
- Associate parents with students.
- Support multiple guardians.
- Improve institute-parent communication.
- Provide academic transparency.
- Support parent engagement.
- Maintain parent relationship history.

---

# Business Entities

The following business entities belong to the Parent Management domain.

---

## Parent

### Purpose

Represents a parent or legal guardian responsible for monitoring and supporting a student's academic journey.

### Business Responsibilities

- Monitor student attendance.
- Review academic performance.
- View assessment results.
- Receive institute announcements.
- Communicate with the institute.

---

## Parent Profile

### Purpose

Represents the complete profile of a parent or guardian.

### Business Responsibilities

- Maintain personal information.
- Maintain contact information.
- Maintain communication preferences.
- Maintain emergency contact details.

---

## Parent-Student Association

### Purpose

Represents the relationship between parents and students.

A student may have multiple guardians, and a parent may be associated with multiple students.

### Business Responsibilities

- Associate parents with students.
- Maintain guardian relationships.
- Support primary and secondary guardians.
- Maintain relationship history.

---

## Parent Communication

### Purpose

Represents communication between parents and the coaching institute.

### Business Responsibilities

- Receive announcements.
- Receive academic notifications.
- Receive attendance alerts.
- Receive assessment updates.
- Support two-way communication.

---

## Parent Notifications

### Purpose

Represents important updates delivered to parents regarding student activities.

### Examples

- Student Absent
- Test Result Published
- New Announcement
- Holiday Notice
- Parent Meeting
- Assignment Due
- Fee Reminder

### Business Responsibilities

- Deliver timely notifications.
- Keep parents informed.
- Improve academic engagement.

---

## Parent Meeting

### Purpose

Represents scheduled meetings between institute representatives and parents.

### Business Responsibilities

- Schedule meetings.
- Discuss academic progress.
- Review performance.
- Plan improvement strategies.

# Business Principles

- Every parent should be associated with at least one student.
- A student may have multiple guardians.
- Parents should access only their associated students.
- Parent information should remain independent from student records.
- Communication should be secure and role-based.
- Parent engagement should continue throughout the student's academic journey.
