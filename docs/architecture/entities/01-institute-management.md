# Institute Management Domain

## Purpose

The Institute Management domain is responsible for managing the operational and administrative information of a coaching institute.

This domain serves as the foundation for configuring how an institute operates within the platform. It defines institute identity, academic structure, operational settings, working schedules, and institutional policies required for day-to-day management.

The primary objective of this domain is to ensure that every coaching institute can operate independently while following its own academic and administrative configurations.

---

# Objectives

- Manage institute information.
- Configure academic years.
- Maintain institute branches.
- Define working schedules.
- Manage holidays and events.
- Configure institute branding.
- Support multi-branch expansion.
- Standardize institute operations.

---

# Business Entities

The following business entities belong to the Institute Management domain.

---

## Institute

### Purpose

Represents a coaching institute operating on the platform.

Each institute maintains its own students, tutors, academic structure, assessments, learning resources, and administrative settings.

### Business Responsibilities

- Maintain institute profile.
- Manage institute operations.
- Configure institute preferences.
- Monitor institute activities.
- Maintain institute identity.

---

## Branch

### Purpose

Represents a physical or virtual branch of a coaching institute.

A coaching institute may operate from multiple locations while sharing the same platform.

### Business Responsibilities

- Organize branch operations.
- Assign students and tutors.
- Maintain branch information.
- Support branch-level reporting.

> Phase 1: Single Branch Supported
>
> Future Phase: Multi-Branch Management

---

## Academic Year

### Purpose

Represents an academic session during which coaching programs are conducted.

Academic years help organize courses, batches, assessments, and reports.

### Business Responsibilities

- Define academic session.
- Organize yearly operations.
- Archive previous academic years.
- Support historical reporting.

---

## Working Calendar

### Purpose

Represents the institute's operational calendar.

The working calendar defines regular working days, holidays, examination periods, and important academic events.

### Business Responsibilities

- Define working days.
- Manage institute holidays.
- Plan academic schedules.
- Coordinate institute activities.

---

## Holiday

### Purpose

Represents official holidays observed by the institute.

Holidays are considered while preparing timetables, scheduling classes, and planning examinations.

### Business Responsibilities

- Maintain holiday calendar.
- Avoid scheduling conflicts.
- Notify students and tutors.

---

## Institute Branding

### Purpose

Represents the institute's visual identity displayed throughout the platform.

### Business Responsibilities

- Configure institute logo.
- Configure institute name.
- Configure contact information.
- Configure branding assets.
- Maintain institute identity.

---

## Institute Policies

### Purpose

Represents operational rules and policies followed by the institute.

These policies guide academic operations and communication with students, parents, and tutors.

### Examples

- Attendance Policy
- Assessment Policy
- Leave Policy
- Fee Policy
- Communication Policy

# Business Principles

- Every coaching institute operates independently.
- Every institute maintains its own academic data.
- Every institute has its own academic calendar.
- Institute settings should not affect other institutes.
- Academic years organize institute operations.
- Holidays should be considered during scheduling.
- The platform should support future multi-branch expansion.
